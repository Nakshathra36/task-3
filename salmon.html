Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  3. Brush the salmon fillets with the mixture.
  4. Grill salmon for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. Remove from the grill and garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite side dish!
