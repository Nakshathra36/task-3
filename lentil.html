Lentil Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and carrots. Saut√© for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and let simmer for 20-25 minutes until lentils are soft.
  6. Blend slightly for a creamy texture (optional).
  7. Serve hot, garnished with cilantro and lemon wedges.
